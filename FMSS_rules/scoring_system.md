# FMSS Scoring System & Awards

**Standard 5-a-Side Football Rules with Brotherhood Bonuses**

---

## Match Scoring

### Points System

| Event | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Goal Scored | 1 point |
| Victory (Win) | 3 points |
| Draw | 1 point each |
| Loss | 0 points |

### Example
- **Win 4-2:** 3 points + 4 goals = 4 points total
- **Draw 3-3:** 1 point + 3 goals = 4 points total
- **Loss 1-3:** 0 points + 1 goal = 1 point total

---

## League Rankings

### Standings Table
Tracks across all matches:
- **Matches Played (MP)**
- **Wins (W)**
- **Draws (D)**
- **Losses (L)**
- **Goals For (GF)**
- **Goals Against (GA)**
- **Goal Difference (GD)**
- **Total Points (Pts)**

### Tiebreakers (in order)
1. Most points
2. Best goal difference
3. Most goals scored
4. Head-to-head record
5. Fair play (fewest fouls/incidents)

## Individual Awards

### Seasonal Awards 🏆

**Top Scorer**
- Most goals in season
- Bragging rights! ⚽

**Best Goalkeeper**
- Fewest goals conceded (if same goalkeeper)
- Most impressive saves

**Fairest Player**
- Voted by teammates
- Embodies "Love and Love Only"
- No fouls, great sportsmanship

**Most Improved**
- Biggest skill improvement over season
- Great for motivating newer players

**Brotherhood Award** ❤️
- Player who showed most support for teammates
- Great attitude on and off pitch
- Voted by players

**Best Team Play**
- Most assists or team goals
- Encourages passing and teamwork

---

## Match Day Awards

### Goals
- **Hat-trick:** 3+ goals in one match
- **Brace:** 2 goals in one match
- **Goal of the Match:** Best scored goal (voted)

### Performances
- **Best Player of Match:** Voted by opposing team
- **Man of the Match:** Voted by teammates

---

## Season Structure

### Example Season (Casual Format)
- **Duration:** 3-4 months (e.g., June-August)
- **Matches:** 1-2 per week
- **Total Regular Season:** 8-12 matches per player
- **Final:** Championship match between top 2 teams

### Playoff Format (if desired)
- **Top 2 teams** play final match
- **Winner:** Season champions 🏆
- **Celebration:** Team breakfast/dinner together

## Important Notes

⚠️ **This is casual football** - awards are for fun and motivation, not life-or-death!

✅ **Everyone celebrates:**
- Even 1 goal = celebration
- Good pass = appreciation
- Effort = respect

❌ **Not about:**
- Individual glory
- Harsh competitiveness
- Excluding less-skilled players
