# FMSS League Rules

**Motto:** Love and Love Only - Brotherhood in Every Match

---

## Game Format

- **Type:** 5-a-side or 6-a-side Football
- **Venues:** 
  - 365 Sport (Dubai)
  - Al Wasl Sports Club (Dubai)
- **Match Duration:** 60 minutes (1 hour)

---

## Playing Rules

### General Principles
- **Friendly First:** This is a casual, brotherhood-focused league. Keep it fun and friendly.
- **No Offside:** Offside rule does NOT apply. Striker-heavy play is encouraged!
- **Casual Fouls:** Easy on fouls. We're mates playing together, not professionals.
  - No harsh penalization for minor contact
  - Focus on keeping the game flowing
  - Dangerous play is still discouraged

### Ball Control
- Standard 5-a-side football rules apply where not overridden by above
- Throw-ins or kick-ins as per venue
- Corner kicks and free kicks from fouls

### Scoring
- Goals count as 1 point each
- No penalty kicks (too intense for casual play)
- Free kicks awarded for serious fouls only

---

## Sportsmanship

✅ **Expected:**
- Respect all players regardless of skill level
- Help players up after falling
- Friendly competition
- Have fun and enjoy brotherhood

❌ **Not Tolerated:**
- Dangerous play or intentional fouling
- Disrespect or aggressive language
- Unsafe play that risks injury

---

## Substitutions & Team Size

- **5-a-side Teams:** 5 players on field minimum
- **6-a-side Teams:** 6 players on field minimum
- **Substitutes:** Allowed at any time (rolling substitutes)
- Encourage everyone to play

---

**Remember:** Love and Love Only. Brotherhood over winning. 🤝❤️
