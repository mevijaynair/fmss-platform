## ☕ The Breakfast Rule - "Late = Treat for the Team"

### The Rule
**If you arrive after kick-off time, you buy breakfast for the entire team!**

### Details

| Arrival Time | Consequence |
|-------------|------------|
| On time | All good! Play! ⚽ |
| 1-15 mins late | You're buying breakfast 🍳 |
| 16+ mins late | You're buying breakfast + drinks ☕🥤 |
| More than 30 mins late | You're buying breakfast + lunch prep for next match 😄 |

### How It Works

1. **Match Kickoff Time:** Posted in WhatsApp (usually Friday/Saturday morning)
2. **You're running late:** Message the group immediately
3. **After Match:** Team decides where to eat
4. **You Pay:** Breakfast, coffee, juice for everyone
5. **You're Forgiven:** But next time... be on time! 😄

### The Spirit

- 🤝 **It's bonding:** Breakfast together after the match is sacred
- ❤️ **Brotherhood:** You support the team by feeding them
- 😄 **Fun:** Not punishment, just league humor
- 🎉 **Celebration:** Whether you played or just joined for food
- 💪 **Accountability:** Encourages everyone to be reliable

### Examples

**Scenario 1:**
- Kickoff: 7:00 AM
- You arrive: 7:12 AM
- Result: You buy breakfast for 8 teammates = ~AED 120-200

**Scenario 2:**
- You message: "Stuck in traffic, will be 20 mins late"
- You arrive: 7:20 AM
- Result: Breakfast + drinks for the team!

**Scenario 3:**
- You message: "Can't make it today"
- You don't attend
- Result: No penalty! (But you'll be missed! ❤️)

## 🎯 Additional Brotherhood Rules

### The Welcome Rule 🤝
- **New players always eat for free** on their first match
- Organizer covers their breakfast
- Make them feel part of the family immediately
- Get them integrated with the squad

### The Injury Support Rule 💪
- If a teammate gets injured and **misses multiple matches**:
  - Team visits them (bring food/juice)
  - Check on their recovery
  - Support their comeback

### The Comeback Rule 🔄
- Players returning after injury/absence:
  - No judgment, just glad to have them back
  - Start easy, build up fitness
  - Team adapts to help them get up to speed

### The Mentorship Rule 👨‍🏫
- **Experienced players mentor new ones**
- Teach skills, strategy, positioning
- On-field coaching during matches
- Help them gain confidence

### The Respect Rule 🙏
- **No player is too good to:**
  - Help a struggling teammate
  - Play defense
  - Support others
- Ego has no place in FMSS

### The Celebration Rule 🎉
- **Every goal celebrated equally**
  - 1-1 or 10-0, all goals count!
  - All scorers celebrated in group
  - No goal is "too easy"

### The Inclusion Rule ❤️
- **Everyone who shows up plays**
  - No one sits on bench
  - Rolling substitutes, everyone gets time
  - New/weaker players get equal opportunity

## Special Traditions 🦅

### Post-Match Ritual
1. **Final whistle:** Everyone stops
2. **Circle up:** All players together
3. **Appreciation:** Acknowledge good plays from both teams

5. **Breakfast:** Head to cafe as team
6. **Banter:** Share stories, jokes, memories
7. **Hydration:** Coffee, juice, food

- 🏆 All awards announced in group
- 📸 Photo with trophy (or funny mock trophy)
- 🎤 Shoutout for each winner

- ⚽ Easy first match (don't overwhelm)
