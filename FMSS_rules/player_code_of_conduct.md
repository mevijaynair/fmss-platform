# FMSS Player Code of Conduct

**Core Value:** Love and Love Only - We are brothers first, competitors second.

---

## Expected Behavior

### On the Field ⚽

✅ **Play with Integrity:**
- Respect all teammates and opponents
- Accept referee/organizer decisions without argument
- Play fairly and honestly
- Admit when you commit a foul

✅ **Respect for Others:**
- No aggressive language or trash talk
- Help teammates and opponents up after falling
- Congratulate good plays by either team
- Support teammates throughout the match

✅ **Safety First:**
- Report injuries immediately
- Don't play injured (unless minor)
- Warn others of hazards
- Never intentionally injure anyone

❌ **Never:**
- Push, punch, or deliberately hurt opponents
- Use abusive language
- Disrespect the referee/organizer
- Discriminate based on any grounds
- Play dangerously or recklessly

---

### Off the Field 🤝

✅ **Brotherhood Standards:**
- Arrive on time (see Late Breakfast Rule below)
- Communicate via WhatsApp group
- Support teammates in life, not just football
- Celebrate together as family
- Help new players feel welcome

❌ **Not Acceptable:**
- Not informing organizer if you can't come
- Creating drama or conflict
- Excluding players from the group
- Negative talk about the league or players

---

## Late Breakfast Rule ☕🍳

**If you arrive late to the match, you buy breakfast for the team!**

- Arrival after kick-off time = breakfast duty
- This is a fun way to build brotherhood
- Organizer decides what/where to eat
- It's all in good spirit! 😄

---

## Consequences for Misconduct

1. **First Offense:** Verbal warning from organizer
2. **Second Offense:** Sit out one match
3. **Repeated Violations:** Removal from the league

*Remember: We're brothers. Rules exist to protect that bond, not to punish.*

---

## Code Summary

**WE PLAY WITH:**
- ❤️ Love and respect
- 🤝 Brotherhood and unity
- ⚽ Skill and fair play
- 😄 Fun and friendship
- 🙏 Gratitude for being together

**Love and Love Only.** 🦅
