FMSS Pitch Rules: Restarts & Time-Wasting
​Let's keep the game flowing and cut out the "dark arts." Moving forward, please stick to these clear rules:
​🚫 No Time-Wasting: If it's not your ball, leave it. Absolutely no kicking the ball away or holding onto it to kill momentum.
​⏱️ Quick Restarts (No Milking the Clock): Play should restart immediately. A 10-second limit applies if the ball rolls far away, but it is not a target to stall. Intentionally walking slowly or milking the clock will be called out.
​📏 The 2-Step Rule: Give the taker at least two big steps of space immediately. Do not stand directly over the ball to block a quick restart.
​🚩 Corner Defending: Give the 2 steps of distance. You can block the inner passing line, but do not stretch to block the outer line and the keeper at the same time.
​🤝 Enforcement: Captains will actively manage this. Deliberate delays, stalling, or refusing to give distance will result in losing possession or giving the attack a better free kick.
​Let's rely on actual defending and keep the standard high!
