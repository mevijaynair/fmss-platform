# FMSS Match Procedures

**How We Organize Our Friendly Games**

---

## Match Announcement

1. **Organizer posts in WhatsApp group:**
   - Date & Time
   - Venue (365 Sport or Al Wasl Dubai)
   - Expected number of players
   - Deadline to confirm attendance

2. **Players respond with:** ✅ (confirmed) or ❌ (can't come)

3. **Late cancellations:** Players notify group ASAP if they can't make it

---

## Before the Match

### Team Selection (30 mins before)
- Organizer arrives early to confirm venue access
- Teams picked fairly (try to balance skill levels)
- Friendly suggestion: Mix experienced and new players
- Discuss any injuries or concerns

### Setup (15 mins before)
- Check pitch condition
- Agree on ball and equipment
- Brief players on any special rules for that match
- Warm up together (brother bonding time!)

---

## During the Match

### Match Flow
- **Kick-off time:** Exact time announced in WhatsApp
- **Duration:** 1 hour continuous (can take 5-10 min break midway)
- **Substitutions:** Rolling - anyone can swap at any time
- **Referee:** Organizer or agreed-upon player calls fouls fairly

### Match Protocol
1. Coin toss for kickoff direction
2. Start match at agreed time
3. Casual fouls handled by agreement (no cards)
4. Breaks as needed for water/rest
5. Final whistle at 60 minutes

---

## After the Match

### Immediate
- ✋ High-fives all around
- 💧 Water and cool down
- 📱 Celebrate on WhatsApp
- 📸 Post match photos (if available)

### Within 24 Hours
- **Organizer updates scoreline** in WhatsApp
- Players can comment on highlights
- Discuss next match date
- Give shoutouts to great players

### Feedback
- Venue feedback (was it good?)
- Any injuries to monitor?
- Suggestions for next match format

---

## Match Cancellation Policy

**Match is cancelled if:**
- Venue is unavailable
- Weather makes pitch unplayable
- Less than 8 players confirm (check with organizer)
- Emergency situation

**Notification:** WhatsApp group alert with 2+ hours notice if possible

---

## Communication Through WhatsApp

### Important Channels
- **Main Group:** General announcements, jokes, brotherhood banter
- **Match Updates:** Date, time, teams, scores
- **Emergency:** Injuries, late cancellations
- **Celebration:** Goals, great plays, funny moments

### Group Rules
- Be respectful in chat (same as on field)
- No spam or off-topic drama
- Share match videos/photos for memories
- Celebrate each other's goals and efforts

---

## Special Match Types

### Friendly Scrimmage
- Pick 5 vs 5 or 6 vs 6
- Casual format, focus on fun
- Ideal for new players to integrate

### Championship Match
- Most competitive format
- Still casual, but everyone tries their best
- Extra bragging rights for winners! 😄

### Charity/Fun Match
- Mixed teams for maximum fun
- Perhaps play with different rules
- Raises community spirit

---

## Organizing Your Own Match

**Any player can organize!**
1. Pick a date, time, and venue
2. Post in WhatsApp with details
3. Wait for confirmations
4. Arrange pitch access
5. Arrive 30 mins early
6. Run the match!

**That's it. Simple and beautiful.** ⚽🤝
