# FMSS Rules & League Guidelines

**League Motto:** Love and Love Only - Brotherhood in Every Match

---

## 📋 Official Documents

### 1. [League Rules](league_rules.md) ⚽
- **Game format:** 5-a-side or 6-a-side
- **Venues:** 365 Sport & Al Wasl Dubai
- **Duration:** 1 hour matches
- **Key rule:** No offside, casual fouls, friendship first
- **Best for:** Understanding the game fundamentals

### 2. [Player Code of Conduct](player_code_of_conduct.md) 🤝
- **On-field behavior:** Respect, safety, fair play
- **Off-field standards:** Punctuality, teamwork, community
- **The Late Breakfast Rule ☕:** Arrive late? You buy breakfast!
- **Consequences:** Progressive warnings for misconduct
- **Philosophy:** Rules protect our brotherhood

### 3. [Match Procedures](match_procedures.md) 📱
- **How we organize:** Via WhatsApp group coordination
- **Before match:** Team selection, setup, warm-up
- **During match:** Substitutions, communication, breaks
- **After match:** Celebration, photo sharing, next match planning
- **Special formats:** Scrimmages, championships, charity matches
- **Best for:** Understanding day-to-day operations

### 4. [Scoring System & Awards](scoring_system.md) 🏆
- **Match points:** Standard 5-a-side scoring
- **League standings:** Rankings and tiebreakers
- **Individual awards:** Top Scorer, Fairest Player, Brotherhood Award
- **Match awards:** Goal of the Match, Player of the Match
- **Season structure:** Regular season + playoffs
- **Philosophy:** Awards for fun and motivation, not competition

### 5. [Special Rules & Traditions](special_rules.md) 🎉
- **☕ Breakfast Rule:** Late arrivals buy breakfast for team
- **Brotherhood traditions:** Post-match rituals, welcome newcomers
- **Special events:** Season opener, championship, charity matches
- **WhatsApp culture:** How we communicate and support
- **The Code of Brotherhood:** Our core values and pledge
- **Best for:** Understanding FMSS culture and traditions

## 🦅 FMSS Philosophy

**We are not a professional league.** We are brothers who love football and love each other.

- ❤️ **Love first:** Love the game, love your brothers
- 🤝 **Inclusive:** Everyone plays, all skill levels welcome
- 😄 **Fun-focused:** Winning is nice, friendship is everything
- 💪 **Supportive:** We lift each other up on and off pitch
- 🙏 **Respectful:** Zero tolerance for disrespect, always respect
